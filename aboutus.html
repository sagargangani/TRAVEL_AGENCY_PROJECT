Trail Finders

About Us

We’re a North American travel company named Trail Finders established in Canada , that specializes in flight, tours, and vacation packages.


Here at Trip Support, we believe that travel should be affordable for all.


By establishing positive and lasting relationships with clients, utilizing state-of-the-art technology, and providing service beyond reproach, we make that belief a reality.

Our Mission


Our mission at Trip Support is to provide its clients with the best convenient and economical solutions for their travel and vacation requirements.


Trip Support uses up-to-date technology and financing tools that provide clients with the best possible travel solutions. We offer high-quality, personalized, and tailor-made services and packages at the best possible prices. Trip Support prides itself on its outstanding client care, best available rates, deals, and easy payment methods. With these tools and skills, we establish positive and lasting relationships with clients. We are committed to providing our guests with the best possible travel and vacations experiences.


Trip Support offers a unique travel financing service program that makes payments easy for everyone. We use a “Book Now, Pay Later” approach, where payments can be made in easy installments. Uniquely, we offer this approach without the need for credit checks and extra boring, time-consuming documentation. We believe that people should be able to travel regardless of their credit score.

Our Brand


Trail Finders is an incorporation that was established in 2023 in Toronto, Canada. In Future, we will expand our service to American travelers by establishing a new branch in California.


Trail Finders is one of the fastest-growing travel agencies in North America. At the core of our brand, we believe in making travel simple and easy for all our clients. Today, our multinational business structure is a testament to its CEO’s long-standing vision of making “travel affordable for all.”
