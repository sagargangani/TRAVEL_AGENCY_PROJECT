Dazzling Dubai

Welcome to the playground of the rich and famous! Dubai is bigger, grander, more lavish (and considerably more expensive) than any other place on Earth. One of the world's most expensive hotel, the Burj Al Arab, is a universally recognized sailboat-shaped architectural wonder; the world's tallest building, the Burj al Khalifa, rises high in the city's center; an entire series of man-made islands, in the shape of palm fronds and another series in the shape of the entire world, are being created off the shore line. In true Friendly Planet style, your 6-day vacation package to Dubai includes airfare, deluxe hotel accommodations, meals, transfers, comprehensive city tours with English-speaking guides, and more!

Countries of Spain and Portugal, Europe

Dive into this incredible journey along the Douro River Valley, punctuated with rolling vineyards and charming native farms that create a breathtaking backdrop to one of Europe's most unspoiled regions and Port country— the world's oldest demarcated wine region. Indulge in authentic culinary experiences and sample world-renowned wines at historic wineries and local quintas (wine estates), from Espumante, a Portuguese sparkling wine, and Port-wine to vinho do Porto, made exclusively in the Douro Valley. Learn about the stages of wine production at the Quinta da Avessada, home to an interactive museum commemorating the history and culture of wine.

Majestic Niagara and Beyond

From majestic mountains to sprawling prairies and picturesque lakes, the breathtaking landscapes of Canada are as diverse as the many cultures that make up this great nation. You’ll find beautiful scenery and a wide range of things to explore from coast to coast, with endless adventures to be had for every travel style. Embark on a hiking, canoeing or camping excursion in one of the country’s 46 national parks and 1,000 provincial parks. Whether you want to explore the serene fjords of Gros Morne National Park in Newfoundland and Labrador, ski down the snowy peaks of Mount-Tremblant National Park in Quebec or paddle across the crystal-clear waters of Jasper National Park in Alberta, there’s something for everyone!

California Dreaming

From the Hollywood Hills to the Golden Gate Bridge and Big Sur, there is so much to discover in California. Located on the west coast, this U.S. State offers something for every travel style. Hike through the mountains of Yosemite National Park, go wine tasting in Napa Valley, spot celebrities in Beverly Hills, drive along the Pacific Coast Highway or spend days at the beach – there are endless adventures to be had in the Golden State.
