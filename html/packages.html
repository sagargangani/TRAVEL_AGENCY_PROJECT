Countries of Spain and Portugal, Europe

Dive into this incredible journey along the Douro River Valley, punctuated with rolling vineyards and charming native farms that create a breathtaking backdrop to one of Europe's most unspoiled regions and Port country— the world's oldest demarcated wine region. Indulge in authentic culinary experiences and sample world-renowned wines at historic wineries and local quintas (wine estates), from Espumante, a Portuguese sparkling wine, and Port-wine to vinho do Porto, made exclusively in the Douro Valley. Learn about the stages of wine production at the Quinta da Avessada, home to an interactive museum commemorating the history and culture of wine.
